Escaneja els productes alimentaris per a mascotes o el seu codi de barres per obtenir dades sobre ingredients, additius i dades nutricionals.

For human food, you can install Open Food Facts, for pet food, Open Pet Food Facts, and for all other products, Open Products Facts.

L’aplicació permet visualitzar els més de 3300 productes d’aliments per a mascotes que ja contenen la base de dades oberta i gratuïta Open Pet Food Facts, i també afegir imatges i dades dels productes que falten.

Per descobrir el projecte Open Pet Food Facts, visiteu https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org

*** Contribute to Pet Food Transparency ***

- Open Pet Food Facts is a pet food products database made by everyone, for everyone.
- You can use it to make better pet food choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
- Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. Tenim molts projectes interessants en els que hi podeu contribuir de moltes maneres diferents. 
- You can start contributing today by adding a product from your pet's cupboard.

▶ Get the Facts

You will be able to find:
- list of ingredients (including ash content)
- nutritional info, nutrition traffic lights…
- brands, allergens, labels (bio, gluten free, vegan, vegetarian, halal, kosher ...), traceability information (packaging codes, origins of ingredients)
- It works with dog food (dry dog food & wet dog food), cat food, rabbit food, snake food, fish food, bird food, horse food or any food for your favorite animal.

*** Works well in any condition ***
- Podeu afegir nous productes, fins i tot si ara mateix no teniu una connexió a Internet.
- The app loads only the strict necessary to reduce data and battery consumption.
- You don't need a camera to look up a product. You can also type the barcode if you lack a camera or have an issue with it.

*** Your pet food, your data ***
- Your data is yours, and is never sent online
- Podeu utilitzar l'aplicació de forma anònima
- Podeu exportar el vostre historial d'escaneig
