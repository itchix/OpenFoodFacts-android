*** Ka ba da gudummawa wajen tabbatar da kwaskwarima ***

- Bugun Labaran Lissafi Kwarewar kayan kwalliyar kayan kwaskwarima wanda kowa yayi, domin kowa.
- Kuna iya amfani da shi don yin zaɓin mafi kyawun kayan kwalliya, kuma kamar yadda yake buɗe bayanan, kowa na iya sake amfani dashi don kowane dalili.
- Bugun Kayan Fata na Kyauta wani shiri ne wanda ba riba ba wanda dubunnan masu ba da agaji na duniya suka kirkira. We have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways. 
- Kuna iya fara ba da gudummawa a yau ta ƙara samfurin daga akwatin kyan kwalliyar ku. 

*** Get the Facts ***

- Kayan shafawa, Fil. lambobi, CI-lambobi, INCI (Nasashen Duniya na Kayan kwaskwarima), jerin abubuwan samarwa, abubuwan ƙira, lambobin shiryawa... 
- Bude kyawawan Labaran Shakewa yana taimaka maka wajen sanya kyakkyawan ma'anar buga takardu akan abubuwan samfuri.
Ba kwa buƙatar zama chemist ba ko kuma ku koyi latin babu kuma don fitar da jerin abubuwan da ake amfani da su :-)
- Kuna iya bincika: aftershaves, lotions na jiki, creams, deodorant, eyeliner, tushe, kamshi, lipstick, makeup, mask, turare, shamfu, sabulu, sabulun wanka, karin, da abun gyaran farce, da sauransu…
- Muna tsammanin zai iya zama da amfani ga mutanen da ke da yanayin fata kamar ƙaiƙayi, psoriasis, eczema… don yin zaɓin mafi kyawun kayan kwalliya.

*** Yana aiki da kyau a kowane yanayi ***
- Kuna iya ƙara sabbin samfura, koda kuwa ba ku da haɗin Intanet a yanzu.
- The app loads only the strict necessary to reduce data and battery consumption
- You don't need a camera to look up a product. You can also type the barcode if you lack a camera or have an issue with it.
