For human food, you can install Open Food Facts, for cosmetics, Open Beauty Facts, and for all other products, Open Products Facts.
But what about other products ? Az Open Food Facts élelmiszerek nyílt adatbázisa, a termékekhez tartozó összetevők, allergének, tápanyagok adataival együtt és egyéb információval, ami a termék csomagolásán vagy címkéjén szerepel.

***Contribute to Pet Food Transparency***

- Open Pet Food Facts is a pet food products database made by everyone, for everyone.
- You can use it to make better pet food choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
- Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. Rengeteg izgalmas projektünk van, amelyekkel sokféleképpen járulhat hozzá. 
- You can start contributing today by adding a product from your pet's cupboard. 

*** Collect everything ***
- Do you have a collection of products with barcodes ? You can add all of it to Open Products Facts
- Open Crayons Facts - The largest open database of coloring crayons
- Open Phone Facts - The largest open database of mobile phones
- Create your own open database

*** Your pet food, your data ***
- Your data is yours, and is never sent online
- You can use the app anonymously and export your scan history
