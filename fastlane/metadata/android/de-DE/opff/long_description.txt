Tierfutter erfassen oder deren Strichcode einlesen, um Daten über Inhaltsstoffe, Zusatzstoffe und Nährwertangaben zu erhalten.

The app allows to view the over 600 pet food products already contained in the free and open database Open Pet Food Facts, and also to add pictures and data for missing products.

Um das Projekt „Open Pet Food Facts” zu entdecken, besuchen Sie bitte https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org

Was Sie ebenfalls finden können (entsprechend den Angaben auf den Produkten):
- list of ingredients (including ash content
• Nährwertangaben, Nährwertampeln …
• Marken, Allergene, Etiketten (Bio, Vegan …)


You can scan dog food (dry dog food & wet dog food), cat food, rabbit food, snake food, fish food, bird food, horse food or any food for your favorite animal.


For human food, you can install Open Food Facts, for cosmetics, Open Beauty Facts, and for all other products, Open Products Facts.
